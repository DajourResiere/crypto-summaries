BitConnect is an open source Bitcoin and crypto community developed by an unknown programmer, or a group of programmers, using their own cryptocurrency BitConnect Coin for all BitConnect lending operations. The price of BitConnect was 0.17 USD in January 2017, and as of august 2017 it trades for more than $50 dollars, increasing a 1800% value in the first 6 months. BitConnect coin released as open source software in January 2017
